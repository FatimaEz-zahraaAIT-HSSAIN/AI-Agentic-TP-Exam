WORKEASE CORPORATE LEAVE POLICY
1. Annual Leave: All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. 
2. Sick Leave: Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.
3. Maternity/Paternity Leave: Employees are entitled to 12 weeks of paid family leave following the birth or adoption of a child.
4. Unapproved Absence: Any absence not logged in the WorkEase system and approved by a direct manager will be considered unapproved. The first unapproved absence results in a written warning. A second offense may result in suspension.
5. Carry-over: Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 days of unused annual leave to the following calendar year.
